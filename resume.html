Achieving a perfect score in written communication in the General Assessment Task (GAT, 2016) and an A+ outcome in VCE English at Frankston High School, I began a marketing course at RMIT university in which I became an expert in the Microsoft suite and developed an understanding of adobe, HTML, computing and marketing principles.

During this time, I developed a blog and wrote at any given moment possible. I earned a position as a content writer at a start up media company, Vicious Vibes.
